Comparing Results of Audio Files Recorded Ourselves

We also recorded audio files ourselves, and wanted to see how our models performed on those. For a self-recorded clip of playing a bellset with the air conditioning in the background, the U-Net actually did not perform as well as it did on files from the online repository. We are still exploring reasons as to why, but we believe it may be due to differnces in the AC noise in the self-recorded clip and the AC noise that was used during training.
